Captain America

Captain America was the first Marvel Comics character to appear in media outside comics with the release of the 1944 movie serial, Captain America.
Since then, the character has been featured in other films and television series.
In the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU), the character is portrayed by Chris Evans.
Captain America was ranked sixth on IGN's "Top 100 Comic Book Heroes of All Time" in 2011,
second in their list of "The Top 50 Avengers" in 2012, and second in their "Top 25 best Marvel superheroes" list in 2014.
